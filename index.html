twoMouths.js

"twoMouths.js" is a metaphorical digital piece, representing the concept of code-switching. Featuring an ever-changing image backdrop and cryptic verses scrolling across the screen, these elements symbolize the act of switching between vernaculars; the dynamic interplay mirroring the fluidity and adaptability inherent in language, emphasizing the intricacies and requirements of those who code-switch. A simple yet creative exploration of the interplay between programming and linguistic expression.

2024, Digital Altar
